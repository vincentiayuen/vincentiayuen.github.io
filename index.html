Vincentia Yuen

Assistant Professor of Marketing · City University of Hong Kong

My name is Vincentia Yuen. I am an Assistant Professor of Marketing at the City University of Hong Kong. I received my Ph.D. in Business (Marketing) from the Miami Herbert Business School, University of Miami.

I study how consumers interact with technology and make decisions in digital environments. My research interests include consumer-technology interaction, word of mouth and user-generated content, and prosocial behavior.

Contact: vincentia.yuen@cityu.edu.hk

Personal Life

I am originally from Hong Kong and have spent most of my life between Hong Kong and North America. My two favorite cities are Hong Kong and Miami — both vibrant, beautiful places with incredibly diverse cultures that have shaped who I am today.

Like many of my peers, I am an avid social media user — which naturally fuels my research interests in digital consumer behavior, user-generated content, and online word of mouth.
